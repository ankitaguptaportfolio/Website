01 — Identity

About Me

I'm Ankita Gupta, a dual-degree engineer (B.Tech + M.Tech) from Kalinga Institute of Industrial Technology, with over 5 years of hands-on experience building AI and ML systems that solve real world, high-stakes problems.

Currently serving as Head of AI/ML and Software Team at Next Big Innovation Labs, I lead the team responsible for architecting and shipping intelligent systems end-to-end — from data collection and model training to production deployment.

"Structured thinking, long-term vision, and disciplined execution — combining healthcare, technology and operational strategy to create durable, intelligent solutions."

My proudest work to date: Ideating and delivering the world's first In-house Real-time Quality Control system for a bioprinter — integrated directly into TRIVIMA, NBIL's 3D bioprinter. This involved building datasets from scratch, developing scaffold data banks, training custom models, and deploying the full solution into a live medical-grade product.

My approach is rooted in structured thinking, long-term vision, and disciplined execution. I'm drawn to high-impact problems that require cross-domain integration — combining healthcare, technology, and operational strategy to create durable, intelligent solutions.
